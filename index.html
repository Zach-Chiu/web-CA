A coffee bean is a seed from the Coffea plant and the source for coffee.It is the pit inside the red
or purple fruit.This fruit is often referred to as a coffee cherry, and like the cherry, it is a fruit
with a pit.

Even though the coffee beans are not technically beans, they are referred to as such because of their
resemblance to true beans. The fruits most commonly contain two stones with their flat sides together.
A small percentage of cherries contain a single seed, called a "peaberry".
